Featured Vacation: Paris, France

Paris is the capital of France and the city of lights. It is one of the most romantic cities in the world. Some landmarks that can be visited are the Eiffel Tower, Notre Dame de Paris, The Louvre, and Les Invalides. An interesting fact about Les Invalides is that Napoleon lays to rest inside of 6 coffins. There are many famous artworks in The Louvre such as the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, St. John the Baptist, and Borghese Gladiator.
